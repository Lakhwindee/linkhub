# HubLink Platform - Video Script & Demo Guide for Leo

## Platform Overview
**HubLink** is a global tourism-social platform that connects travelers and content creators worldwide. It combines social networking, travel discovery, event planning, and monetization opportunities for creators.

---

## Video Structure (Recommended: 2-3 minutes)

### 1. INTRO (15 seconds)
**What to Show:**
- HubLink landing page with logo
- Tagline: "Connect with millions of travelers worldwide"

**Script:**
"Meet HubLink - the all-in-one platform connecting travelers, creators, and tourism businesses globally. Whether you're exploring the world or building your brand, HubLink has everything you need."

---

### 2. DISCOVER SECTION - Interactive Map (30 seconds)
**What to Show:**
- Navigate to Discover page
- Show the interactive 3D globe/map
- Zoom into different countries (UK, India, USA)
- Click on cities to see local events and activities
- Demonstrate real-time location browsing

**Script:**
"Start your journey on the Discover page. Use our interactive live map to explore countries and cities around the world. Zoom in to any location to find local events, attractions, and connect with travelers in that area."

**Key Actions to Record:**
1. Open Discover page
2. Rotate globe to show different continents
3. Click on a country → Shows cities
4. Click on a city → Shows events and activities
5. Show event details popup

---

### 3. SUBSCRIPTION PLANS (45 seconds)

#### A. For Travelers (Free & Premium)
**What to Show:**
- Navigate to /subscribe page
- Show plan comparison table

**Script:**
"HubLink offers flexible plans for everyone:

**Free Plan:**
- Browse and discover events
- Follow other travelers
- Basic profile features

**Traveler Plan (Premium):**
- Unlock exclusive events
- Priority bookings
- Enhanced profile customization
- Ad-free experience

**Creator Plan ($45/month):**
This is where it gets exciting for content creators..."

#### B. For Creators - Monetization (Important!)
**What to Show:**
- Creator plan benefits
- Campaign marketplace
- Earnings dashboard

**Script:**
"As a Creator on HubLink, you can earn money by promoting brand campaigns in your content:

1. **Browse Active Campaigns**: Brands post advertising campaigns with specific requirements
2. **Pick Campaigns**: Choose ads that match your niche and audience
3. **Create Content**: Include the brand promotion in your videos, posts, or stories
4. **Submit for Approval**: Upload your content for admin review
5. **Get Paid**: Once approved by the admin, earnings are added to your wallet
6. **Minimum Withdrawal**: $50 threshold for payouts via Stripe

This turns your travel content into a revenue stream!"

**Key Actions to Record:**
1. Show Campaign Marketplace page
2. Click on a campaign → Show details (brand name, payment, requirements)
3. Navigate to Creator submissions page
4. Show wallet/billing page with earnings breakdown

## Screen Recording Tips for Leo:

1. **Use Clean Browser**: No extensions, clean cache
2. **Full Screen**: Record in 1920x1080 or higher
3. **Smooth Navigation**: Click slowly, let pages load fully
4. **Cursor Highlight**: Use cursor highlighting for visibility
5. **No Personal Data**: Use demo accounts with generic names
6. **Audio Quality**: Clear voiceover with minimal background noise
7. **B-Roll**: Consider adding travel stock footage for transitions
